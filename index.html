Important Notice

This privacy policy applies specifically to the EduTextMx Chrome Extension and complies with Google Chrome Web Store policies and applicable Mexican educational regulations.

Information We Collect

Data We Collect

  • Educational text fragments: Extracted from official CONALITEG books for educational purposes only
  • Source URL: To identify the official CONALITEG source page
  • Timestamp: When extraction occurs for educational audit
  • User account information: Email, name, CURP (Mexican ID) for age validation
  • Usage data: Number of extractions, pages accessed (for legal compliance)

Data We DON'T Collect

  • Browsing history outside CONALITEG websites
  • Biometric data or unique device identifiers
  • Information from other browser tabs or websites
  • Personal data unrelated to educational use
  • Location data beyond Mexican region validation

How We Use Your Data

Exclusive Educational Purpose

All data is used exclusively for:

  • Facilitating educational content extraction under Mexican Federal Copyright Law Article 148
  • Ensuring compliance with copyright legal limits (750 words maximum, 5 pages maximum)
  • Providing personal educational library functionality
  • Validating exclusive educational use by Mexican students
  • Maintaining legal audit records for regulatory compliance
  • Generating automatic APA bibliographic references

Data Sharing

Parties We Share Data With

  • Educational authorities: When required for legal compliance audits
  • Secure cloud storage: Encrypted backup of user's personal educational library (with explicit consent)
  • No commercial third parties: We never share data for commercial purposes

We NEVER Share With

  • Marketing companies or advertisers
  • Commercial data brokers
  • Social media platforms
  • Non-educational third parties

User Rights and Control

  • Delete all stored data at any time
  • Export personal educational library
  • Uninstall extension removes all local data
  • Access complete audit trail of extractions
  • Contact admin@edutextmx.org for data requests

Children's Privacy Protection

Special Protection for Minors

Since our extension serves Mexican students under 18:

  • CURP validation ensures age-appropriate access
  • Enhanced data protection for minor users
  • No marketing or commercial targeting
  • Automatic enforcement of educational content limits

Legal Framework

Constitutional Foundation

EduTextMx operates under a solid legal framework that combines the constitutional right to education, educational fair use protected by LFDA, and best technological practices.

Main Legal Basis

  • Mexican Constitution Article 3: Constitutional right to education
  • Federal Copyright Law Article 148: Educational fair use
  • Children's Rights Law Article 57: Access to educational technologies
  • UN Convention on Rights of the Child: Educational resource access

Automatic Legal Limits

Our system automatically enforces copyright legal limits:

  • Maximum 750 words per extraction
  • Maximum 5 consecutive pages
  • Maximum 10% of total book content
  • Text-only extraction (no image processing)
